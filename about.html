JONAS is a freelance photographer-cinematographer. Originally from Egersund, Norway, now residing in Melbourne, Australia.


Despite being from a small Norwegian fishing village, he hates seafood, as well as the idea of meat pies, despite however many Australians try to change his mind.


In his works, he aims to tell visually compelling stories through photo and video that connect people, cultures and brands, by bridging art and commerce through stories that celebrate individuality, style and truth. He does this by blending documentary honesty and editorial visuals.


In his spare time, he enjoys hiking and trainspotting, rarely without a camera in hand.
