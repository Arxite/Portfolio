JONAS is a freelance photographer and cinematographer originally from Egersund, Norway, now residing in Melbourne, Australia.


Despite being from a small Norwegian fishing village, he hates seafood as well as the thought of meat pies, despite however many Australians try to change his mind.


He aims to tell visually compelling stories through photo and video that connect people, cultures, and brands by bridging art and commerce. His work blends documentary honesty and editorial visuals.


In his spare time, he enjoys hiking and trainspotting, rarely without a camera in hand.
